# Dynamic Pricing Engine

The `PricingEngine` calculates advisory price adjustments based on forecast conditions.

## Advisory Principle
> [!IMPORTANT]
> The dynamic pricing recommendations generated by this module are strictly advisory. They should NEVER directly modify room category rates in the active database. Final pricing adjustments are dispatched as recommended packages for manager approval.

## Pricing Formula
The dynamic price is formulated as:
$$\text{Recommended Price} = \text{Base Price} \times (1.0 + \text{Markup Percentage})$$

### Markup Rules
- **Occupancy-Based adjustments**:
  - Occupancy forecast $> 85\%$: $+20\%$ markup
  - Occupancy forecast $> 70\%$: $+10\%$ markup
  - Occupancy forecast $< 40\%$: $-10\%$ discount
- **Seasonality adjustments**:
  - Peak season periods: $+15\%$ markup

## Standard Loyalty Discounts
- **Bronze**: $0\%$ discount
- **Silver**: $5\%$ discount
- **Gold**: $10\%$ discount
- **Platinum**: $15\%$ discount
