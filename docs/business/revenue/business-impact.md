# Business Impact & KPIs

The platform exposes real-time indicators through the `/revenue/kpis` endpoint to monitor overall performance.

## Key Performance Indicators

- **Occupancy Rate**: Proportion of available rooms occupied.
- **Average Daily Rate (ADR)**: Average revenue earned per occupied room.
- **Revenue Per Available Room (RevPAR)**: Average revenue earned per available room.
- **Upsell Conversion Rate**: Percentage of guests accepting room upgrades.
- **Cross-sell Conversion Rate**: Percentage of guests purchasing ancillary items (Spa, Restaurant).
- **Forecast Accuracy**: Accuracy rate of the regression models against actual bookings.
